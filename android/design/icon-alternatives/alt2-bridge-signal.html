Design Rationale

Fuses the bridge silhouette with a wifi/signal motif. The three concentric arcs above the bridge deck serve double duty: they echo the arch of a cable-stayed bridge while unmistakably communicating wireless connectivity and device control.

  • Signal arcs immediately communicate "wireless control" to any tech audience
  • Geometric towers and cable lines give the bridge strong structural clarity
  • Ocean-depths gradient (#004D61 → #3D5AFE) is faithful to the existing background XML
  • Bridge arch crops gracefully under round/squircle masks
  • Notification icon (arch + dot) is the most minimal possible — recognizable at 16dp
