Design Rationale

The AI Pulse design drops the bridge metaphor entirely in favor of a pure AI identity. The central "AI core" node with radiating pulse rings communicates autonomous intelligence. Six satellite nodes connected by constellation lines represent the Android devices under AI control.

  • Strongest AI / tech identity — immediately reads as "intelligent system"
  • Perfectly circular layout works without cropping under all mask shapes (round, squircle, square)
  • Blue-to-cyan gradient (#5E72E4 → #00E5FF) connects Wave Blue brand with the app's #00E5FF primary
  • Concentric pulse rings are a universally recognized symbol for "active" / "broadcasting"
  • Notification icon (rings + center dot + cardinal ticks) works even below 16dp
  • If the brand evolves toward pure AI tooling, this icon is future-proof

⚠ Note: This design abandons the wave-bridge brand symbol. Consider whether the brand needs continuity before choosing this option.
